About Us


Brat café was founded in 1992 as a relief hotspot for weary space travellers in need of a quick fix, around a time when space-bound coffee dispensers only provided instant coffee.
We use a selection of the best selected beans located within the galaxy ensuring our guests can stay for a intergalactic moment with their beloved, or just a quick grab-n-go.

Our mission

It's simple!, to provide every coffee enthusiasts with the best beans there is in the universe! (or atleast within our galaxy)
